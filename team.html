About Us What is a Millenium Nucleus?

A Millenium Nucleus is a grant awarded by Iniciativa Millenium from the chilean Ministerio de Economía.

The Millenium Nucleus 'Stochastic Models of Complex and Disordered Phenomena' was recently awarded to our team. Our team is mainly based at PUC-Chile and Universidad de Chile, and includes young members based at other Chilean institutions.
